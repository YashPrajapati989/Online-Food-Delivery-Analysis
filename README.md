## 📈 Key Insights

### Top Revenue Items

- Aloo Paratha generated the highest revenue.
- Fish Curry, Hakka Noodles and Momos crossed ₹200K revenue.
- Paneer-based dishes consistently performed well.

### Restaurant Performance

- Golden Garden recorded the highest orders.
- Spice Palace, Tasty Bistro and Big Table followed closely.
- Golden Diner and Royal Kitchen showed the lowest order volumes.

### Monthly Trends

- May was the highest-performing month.
- January–June maintained strong demand.
- December recorded the lowest orders.

### Top Revenue Cities

- Chennai generated the highest revenue.
- Pune and Bangalore followed closely.
- These cities showed the highest customer spending behavior.
